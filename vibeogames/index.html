The List:


>Super Lesbian Animal RPG

A great, longer than i expected, RPG about Lesbian Animals going on adventures and getting past emotional trauma while also saving the world in the process.
It has a lot more narrative weight than i ever thought it would have, the impact on my soul will be remembered forever.
The soundtrack is great, the comedy is on point. Go play it dummy!


>Katawa Shoujo

A game i didn't expect for me to like, seeing that i mostly play Yuri or Lesbian Visual Novels. However, Katawa shoujo managed to surprise me so, so much.

First off, it's a miracle this game came out this way, it was made from random 4chan contributors in the early days of indie gaming.
Somehow, it really does not feel like a 4chan creation. It genuinely has some of the most heartfelt and respectful discussion and exploration into disability and disabled people that i've seen in a game.

The protagonist, Hisao, is a fully colored character, instead of a cardboard self-insert, and his long self-reflections on how he feels about being disabled and how it changes his view of both himself and the friends and potential lover around him still haunts me in a good way.

Go play it, it's completely free, has fan mobile ports, but be wary of the optional NSFW content.
